# Learning Roadmap

Rr. Hydrology is structured into three progressive learning tiers.

## Level 1 – Fundamental Hydrology
Focus on physical understanding and manual computation.

## Level 2 – Applied Hydrology (AI)
Focus on data-driven validation, prediction, and operational modeling.

## Level 3 – Expert Hydrology (AI Agents)
Focus on automated hydrologic workflows with human-in-the-loop decision support.
